    Rating Peppino's Gelato

    Dubrovnik, Croatia

    Flavour Ratings:

    Good Old Vanilla 8/10

    The best vanilla ice cream I've had all year! A lovely soft gelato texture and strong vanilla flavour. All the portions were a generous serving for a single scoop so worth €3.50. A great start!

    Lady's Lemon Cake 9/10

    Delicous milk base with swirls of tangy lemon curd and crunch cake pieces. The contrast of textures make a fun and fresh treat!

    Lemon Power 10/10

    A beautiful tart lemon flavour with a smooth and creamy texture not usually found in sorbets. I'm still dreaming about it!

    Caramel Goat Milk 6/10

    A lovely milk base but unfortunately I wasn't a fan of the chocolate pieces :(

    Dubrovnik Crème Brûlée 9.5/10

    A creamy custard base with a syrup sauce was a fun and unqiue flavour. I would go back just to try it again!

    Yogurt Pomegrante 9/10

    When I saw this on the menu I knew I had to get it! A tangy yogurt base and sweet pomegrante swirls was a delicous combination- rivals my favourite frozen yogurts!

    Forest Feast and Mango Majesty 10/10

    A lovely fruity combination was a perfect end to a tasty trip. The mixture of the strong and exotic Mango flavour as well as the sweet berries made a refreshing finish to dessert after an exciting day! :P

    "A must vist for a cooling refreshment in Dubrovnik!"- IceCreamRater, 2024

    Other Delicous Dubrovnik Delights!

    Sweet Selection

    With mountains of colourful sweets to rival Willy Wonka Captain Candy is a must visit if you fancy some pick 'n' mix.

    A Seafood Lovers' dream

    As a costal city you are perfectly placed to indulge in fresh seabass and mussels. Perhaps venture to the top of the Jesuit Stairs where Rudjer's mouthwatering grilled calamari awaits.

    Local Ice Cream

    If you don't fancy forking out on artisanal gelato Croatia also offers some excellent cheap ice cream. In local newsagents you will find Šlag a refreshingly creamy milk based ice cream. Just remember to bring a spoon!

    Burek

    This classic savoury pastry is sold in the numerous bakeries found at almost every street corner. Light and flaky my favourite are cheese or if you're feeling healthy cheese and spinach.
